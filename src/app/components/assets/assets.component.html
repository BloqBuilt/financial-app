Assets Info

Assets are items that retain value and can be sold at a future date.

Not every asset is the same, some depreciate value as they age while others increase in value.

Examples of Increasing Assets:

  • Real Estate
  • Financial Investments (Stocks, ETFs, ect.)
  • Collectable Items
  • Unique Items

Examples of Depreciating Assets:

  • Vehicles
  • Boats
  • Common Items

There can be exceptions to these examples.
